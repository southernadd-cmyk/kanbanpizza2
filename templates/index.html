üçï How to Play Kanban Pizza
Objective: Build and bake pizzas to score points.

1. Join a Room: Gather your team (3-5 people). Enter the same Room Name & Password. If you are the leader, create the room first!

The Game Loop:
Step 1: Create Ingredients
Tap buttons to generate ingredients into the shared pool.
Step 2: Build Pizza
Drag ingredients to your builder.
Tip: Once built, press "Submit Pizza" to send it to the oven area.
‚ö†Ô∏è The Oven Rule (Read Carefully!)
The oven is your bottleneck. You can only cook 3 pizzas at a time.
How to Bake:
  1. Load max 3 pizzas.
  2. Switch Oven ON.
  3. Wait 30-45 seconds.
  4. Switch Oven OFF to remove.

Note: You cannot add/remove pizzas while the oven is ON.

Round 2: Collaboration

You gain access to Shared Pizza Builders. You can use anyone's builder. Talk to your team to clear bottlenecks!

Round 3: The Market

Customers send specific orders. You must build exactly what is asked.
Orders include:

  • Ham: 1 base, 1 sauce, 4 ham
  • Pineapple: 1 base, 1 sauce, 4 pineapple
  • Ham & Pineapple: 1 base, 1 sauce, 2 ham, 2 pineapple
  • Light Ham: 1 base, 1 sauce, 1 ham
  • Light Pineapple: 1 base, 1 sauce, 1 pineapple
  • Plain: 1 base, 1 sauce
  • Heavy Ham: 1 base, 1 sauce, 6 ham
  • Heavy Pineapple: 1 base, 1 sauce, 6 pineapple
